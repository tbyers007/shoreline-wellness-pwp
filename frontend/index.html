About Andrea

Meet Andrea Byers

Andrea's work is guided by the belief that the body carries an innate capacity for healing when met with safety, presence, and compassionate care. She brings a gentle, individualized approach to each session, creating space for healing that honors the unique experience of every person and family she serves.

Drawing from craniosacral therapy and other integrative modalities, Andrea supports the nervous system's natural ability to restore balance and resilience. Her work is grounded in listening deeply, moving gently, and trusting the wisdom of the body.
