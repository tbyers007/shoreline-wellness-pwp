PWP Milestone 1

Purpose, Audience, & Goal

Purpose: This project will be a professional wellness website designed to educate visitors about holistic healing services and encourage potential clients to learn more about Shoreline Wellness.

Audience: This site is designed for adults seeking holistic wellness services and natural approaches to stress relief, pain management, and overall well-being. The target audience includes prospective clients, individuals interested in alternative healing therapies, and community members seeking information about wellness services offered by Shoreline Wellness.

Goal: The primary goal of this site is to encourage prospective clients to learn about the services offered by Shoreline Wellness and initiate contact through the website's contact form. The secondary goal is to establish a professional and welcoming online presence that builds trust and credibility with visitors.

Persona

Name: Jennifer Martinez

Age: 42

Profession: Elementary School Teacher

Technology: Jennifer uses a MacBook Air at home for personal research and planning. She primarily browses the internet on her iPhone and regularly uses Google, Facebook, Instagram, and email. She is comfortable using websites and online forms but is not highly technical.

Attitudes and Behaviors: Jennifer values her health and is interested in natural approaches to wellness. She prefers to research services online before making decisions and often reads reviews before contacting a business. She is busy balancing her career, family responsibilities, and personal commitments, so she appreciates websites that are easy to navigate and provide clear information quickly.

Situation: Jennifer has been experiencing ongoing stress, tension headaches, and neck discomfort due to her demanding work schedule. Traditional treatments have provided only temporary relief, and she has become interested in holistic wellness services such as Craniosacral Therapy. While searching online for information about alternative wellness options, she discovers Shoreline Wellness.

Frustrations and Needs: Jennifer is overwhelmed by the amount of wellness information available online and is unsure which services are legitimate and appropriate for her needs. She is looking for trustworthy information, a clear explanation of services, pricing information, and an easy way to contact a practitioner. She wants reassurance that she is making a good decision before scheduling an appointment.

Goals: Jennifer wants to determine whether Shoreline Wellness offers services that can help reduce her stress and improve her overall well-being. She wants to understand what Craniosacral Therapy is, how it may benefit her, and how to schedule a consultation if she feels the practice is a good fit.

Additional Information Needed: Jennifer needs a clear description of services offered, information about the practitioner, potential benefits of treatment, contact information, and a simple method for requesting additional information or scheduling an appointment.

User Story: As a prospective client, I want to learn about Shoreline Wellness and its services so that I can determine whether they are right for me and schedule an appointment.
